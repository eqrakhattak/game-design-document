Gameplay

Objectives

The objectives of the Game of Winter are to provide the player with a cool sense of being in the story of Game Of Thrones and living the character's life for themselves. The player will go through many stages/levels of the game unlocking rewards for each one. The story line will go as in the HBO's Game Of Thrones story line. We will try to mimic and bring from the seasons to life the best we can. The players will play to their deaths.

As Queen Cersei says in season one, "When you play the game of thrones, you win or you die. There is no middle ground."

Game Progressions

Game Mechanics

It includes all controls and interactions within the game, including new weapons, abilities, powers, vehicles, and environmental states or events.
Mechanics for Game Of Winter include movement through arrow keys, sword fighting, dragon fire, horses for travel and battle. Snows and storms.

Experience Duration

It's basically the average time it takes to complete each stage, level, mission (including deaths if applicable) or course (using the most relevant vehicle)
The expercience duration for the levels will be 1 hour.

Ancillary Rewards

These are visual, aural, decorative, etc. Include exciting environmental wonders, fancy visual effects, scripted events, etc. Spectacular environmental wonders and effects up front, new visual rewards are staggered at a pace that keeps the user interested.
In this game, the visuals wouldbe solely depend on the scenes. Most of them would be natural and the only fantasy visuals are dragons and thier fiery breath. The visual affects and enviornmental wonders would alter according to the scenes.

Practical Rewards

These are mostly gameplay relevant. For instance, new game modes, upgrades and practical unlockable content.
This game will bring new reward for completion of each level. It might be an heirloom sword or a new outfit.

Difficulty

Obstacles and NPCs/bosses, risks with respect to player injury/death, weapon depletion, or vehicle/equipment damage or loss.
The difficulty level would be medium. Old weapons would give way to new ones. High risk of life loss if deviated from the path.

In-Game GUI

Game of Winter is a game with slick in-game GUI. It has menus, buttons, scrollbars and other UI elements. Displaying information on a canopy for the player. Levels and scores are also diplayed on the dashboard.
